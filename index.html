Bio

I am currently finishing my Masters in the Visual Machines Group at UCLA, where I am advised by Prof Achuta Kadambi. My research focus is on using physical inductive reasoning to improve data-driven approaches to computer vision tasks. In particular, I am interested in scene understanding and explainable systems. My current project focuses on creating a system to anticipate a car accident before it happens by deconstructing the problem physically.
