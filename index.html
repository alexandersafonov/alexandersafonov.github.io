Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the UCLA Visual Machine Laboratory, advised by Prof. Achuta Kadambi. My research interest lies in blending physics world with deep neural network, where we either use the deep neural network to help us discover the hidden physics or utilize the known physics prior to boost the performance of deep neural network. My goal is to allow us to know about the physical process in our world given visual observations. For now, the targeted application scenario is computational imaging. Prior to UCLA, I worked on computational super-resolution microscopy in Zhejiang university, where I receive the master degree in year 2018.
